The bread are freshly baked the morning of delivery date, thus we require all orders be placed at least a day in advance of the delivery date. For example, if you need the bread to be delivered on November 25th (Saturday), you will need to place your order no later than November 24th (Friday).

As soon as we confirm your order, our bakery will begin the bread preparation process. Therefore, in most cases, we are unable to cancel or modify your order. If you need to make adjustments to your order, please email us at: sales@benignbakery.com to inquire if your order can be modified.

We will be happy to change the delivery address as long as your order has not been delivered or is not on its way to being delivered. This must be done by e-mail, quoting your order confirmation number. Additional rerouting fee maybe charged by the delivery personnel.

All our delivery personnel are company staff, to ensure professionalism and proper on time delivery service. We never use contract drivers or third party drivers for all our deliveries. Our delivery fee includes proper bread packaging, food tampering label, and delivery service.
